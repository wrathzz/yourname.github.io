Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

Holy Username Checker is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The tool runs locally on your device and does not send any user data to our servers. Username strings entered into the tool are used solely to query publicly accessible availability endpoints on third-party platforms and are not logged or retained.

3. Third-party platforms

This tool makes requests to third-party platforms (TikTok, Roblox, Discord) on your behalf. These platforms may log your IP address or other network-level information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies directly.

4. Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. We have no user data to share.

6. Children's privacy

This tool is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the platform where this tool is distributed.
